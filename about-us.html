About US

Welcome to MyPetBnB, Mumbai’s trusted community of 5-star Pet sitters and walkers.

We believe Pets deserve more than cages, they deserve love, comfort and care even when their parents are away. At MyPetBnB, our mission is simple: to create a safe, homely space for every Pet while giving parents peace of mind.

Founded in 2025, MyPetBnB connects Pet parents with verified local sitters and walkers across neighbourhoods in Mumbai. Whether it’s for a few hours, a weekend or an extended trip, we make sure your pup is never alone.

We also empower our sitter community with tools, reviews and support to build their own Pet-care businesses on MyPetBnB; backed by the trust and transparency of a growing platform.
